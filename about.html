About Max Ross


I'm a guitarist/singer-songwriter based in Sea Cliff, NY. In addition to performing as a solo artist, I am a member of Dijon (runners-up in the Hard Rock Rising Battle of the Bands), Tedesco (winners of the Mohegan Sun Locals Live contest), and Haley Ganis' band.

In addition to performing, I also maintain a sizable roster of private guitar and piano students and teach through TakeLessons, Kathryn Brickell Music, and the Music Academy of Garden City.
